SOL Clash Whitepaper

SOL Clash is a competitive play-to-earn gaming platform built on the Solana blockchain. Players battle in real-time Rock — Paper — Scissors arenas, earn $CLASH tokens through skill-based performance, and spend them in a living in-game economy — all without ever leaving the platform.

What is SOL Clash?

SOL Clash is built on one idea: your skill should have real value. It is a browser-based arena game where every match is a competitive Rock — Paper — Scissors battle. Win cleanly, earn more. Lose, you still earn something. Every outcome is recorded on-chain and every reward is distributed automatically — no middleman, no manual payouts.

The platform is designed to be accessible to anyone. You do not need a crypto wallet, a browser extension, or any prior blockchain experience. Sign in with Google, GitHub, or Discord and you are ready to play in under a minute. Your Solana wallet is created for you automatically in the background.

▲

Earn by playing

Every match — win, draw, or lose — distributes $CLASH, Battle Points, and EXP. Better performance means better rewards. Skill is the only variable.

◆

Rank up & compete

Progress through 5 rank tiers from Bronze to Diamond. Higher ranks unlock better rewards and put you in contention for the seasonal leaderboard prize pool.

⊞

Own your assets

Arena picks are NFTs on Solana. Your $CLASH balance lives on-chain. Everything you earn is yours — withdraw to any Solana wallet at any time.

🏆

Seasonal prizes

The top 15 players on the seasonal leaderboard share a prize pool of at least 10,000 $CLASH. The pool grows with every marketplace transaction and $CLASH swap.

◈
Core principle

SOL Clash rewards skill, not luck. Every $CLASH token distributed is tied to a verified match outcome — not a random drop or pay-to-win mechanic.

Vision & mission

We believe the future of gaming is player-owned. SOL Clash exists to make that real.

The problem

In traditional gaming, players invest hundreds of hours, build real skills, and generate enormous value — but own nothing. Items live on centralized servers. Accounts can be banned. Earnings disappear when a game shuts down. The value flows one way: from the player to the platform.

◎
The status quo

Millions of competitive game players earn nothing for their time and skill. In-game economies are controlled entirely by the developer, and player assets have no real-world value or portability.

Our solution

SOL Clash flips the model. Every win, every token, every item you earn is yours — on-chain, verifiable, and transferable. The game does not gatekeep your earnings. There are no withdrawal fees hidden in fine print. The rules of the economy are written in this document and enforced by code.

✓

Transparent economy

Every reward formula, every distribution rule, every fee is documented here and applied uniformly. No hidden mechanics, no surprise changes without notice.

◆

Skill-first design

Rewards scale with performance. A perfect win pays more than a standard win. A flawless PvP victory pays more than a close one. Grinding without skill does not pay as well as playing well.

◻

True ownership

Your wallet, your tokens, your NFTs. SOL Clash never holds your funds. Everything is on Solana. You can withdraw, trade, or transfer at any time without permission from the platform.

Our mission

To build a transparent, skill-rewarding arena where the best players are genuinely compensated, and where the in-game economy is governed by fair rules that everyone can read, verify, and trust. SOL Clash is not just a game — it is a proof of concept that competitive gaming and real financial ownership can coexist.

✦
Long-term commitment

SOL Clash is built to grow. Phases 3, 4, and 5 on the roadmap introduce on-chain verification, a public $CLASH token launch, and ecosystem expansion — each step increasing transparency and player ownership.

Fair play & anti-abuse

SOL Clash takes competitive integrity seriously. The reward system includes multiple safeguards to ensure that $CLASH distribution reflects genuine player skill — not exploit loops or automated farming.

AFK & idle detection

Players who go idle mid-match are automatically flagged. Matches completed suspiciously fast are withheld from reward distribution pending review.

RuleDetails
Cooldown between same-pair matches 30-second wait before the same two players can be matched again in PvP
Diminishing returns After 3 matches vs the same opponent within 1 hour, each additional match yields 50% of normal rewards
Suspicious match detection Matches completed in under 5 seconds are flagged and withheld from reward distribution pending review
Repeat-match flagging 5 or more matches against the same opponent in 1 hour triggers an automatic abuse flag for manual review
ℹ

These rules apply equally to all players regardless of rank or account age. No manual overrides exist for individual accounts.

Your wallet

Every SOL Clash account includes a built-in Solana wallet — no external browser extension or third-party app required. Your wallet is generated automatically at signup and is permanently tied to your account.

Self-custody

Your wallet is generated from a 12-word recovery phrase that only you can access. SOL Clash never stores your recovery phrase in plain text — it is encrypted before ever touching our systems. You own your funds.

SOL withdrawals

Withdrawals to any external Solana address are available at any time. A secondary security PIN is required before any outbound transfer is processed, adding a layer of protection against unauthorized withdrawals.

$CLASH balance

Your $CLASH balance is visible on your dashboard and updates in real time after every match. Balances are stored on-chain and reflect your true earning history across all game modes.

◻
No crypto experience needed

SOL Clash handles all blockchain complexity behind the scenes. You interact with a standard gaming interface — the Solana wallet powers everything invisibly in the background.
