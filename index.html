What is SOL Clash?

SOL Clash is a browser-based competitive game where every run through the Flappy Bird pipe arena earns you real in-game rewards. No wallet extension needed. Sign in with Google, GitHub, or Discord, and a Solana custodial wallet is created for you instantly. You can be playing and earning within 60 seconds of first visiting the site.

The game has two modes: Free to Play (practice, no cost, no rewards) and Competitive (costs 1 Energy per run, earns $GOLD, Seeds, Battle Points, and EXP). Rewards are bigger with better Nests and higher player rank.

Game states

Every run follows a fixed state machine: Idle (waiting on start screen) → Ready (bird floats, pipes frozen, waiting for first flap) → Playing (active run) → Dead (collision detected, game-over screen shown).

In the Ready state the bird oscillates with a sine-wave float (sin(frame × 0.06) × 8 px). Pipes are invisible and frozen until the first flap, giving you a moment to orient.

Pipe gaps (competitive)

Each pipe in Competitive mode has a randomly assigned gap size for added difficulty variance. Gap center is also randomised between 25% and 72% of screen height.

The GameEarns Rewards

Competitive mode

Every Competitive run costs 1 Energy and rewards you with $GOLD tokens, Seeds, Battle Points, and EXP — all calculated server-side and credited automatically when the game ends.

What happens in a competitive run

  1. Energy check — Each competitive run costs 1 Energy. If your Energy is at zero, the Play button is disabled and a countdown to the next regen tick is shown.
  2. Collectibles spawn — $GOLD coins (500–1,000 $GOLD each) and Seeds appear in pipe gaps. Spawn rates are governed by the Nests you own.
  3. Score increments — Every pipe cleared = +1 point. Max 9,999 per run.
  4. Game ends — On collision the game tallies your score, collectibles, and run duration. Rewards are calculated and credited to your account automatically.

EXP & leveling

EXP is awarded after every competitive run based on your score and how long the run lasted. Longer, higher-scoring runs yield more EXP. You earn between 1 and 10 EXP per run depending on performance. Each level requires (level + 1) × 200 EXP to complete. Leveling up is cosmetic — it reflects overall experience on the platform.

Game-over screen rewards

After each competitive run the game-over screen displays:

  • Final score and all-time best score
  • EXP bar animation with level-up flash if a new level is reached
  • BP earned this run
  • Seeds collected this run
  • $GOLD collected this run
  • Remaining energy count + countdown to next regen tick
  • Leaderboard button (opens paginated top-player modal)

Regeneration rates by nest rarity

Each Nest you own runs its own independent regen timer tracked in the background. When you open the game or dashboard, elapsed ticks are calculated and credited automatically. More Nests = more parallel ticks = faster total refill.

Nest rarity Regen rate Time to fill 20 slots (solo)

Rare 1 energy per 1 hour 20 hours
Legendary 1 energy per 30 minutes 10 hours

Important rules

  • Timer pauses when full — once at 20 energy, all nest timers pause until you play a game.
  • All timers restart on depletion from full — playing the first game from a full bar restarts all nest timers from that moment.
  • Energy display — shown as a segmented bar on the Dashboard and Play screen. Green = adequate, yellow = low.
  • Zero energy — the Competitive Play button is disabled and shows a countdown: "Next in X h Y m Z s" based on the fastest upcoming nest tick.
⚡ Tip: Owning multiple Nests stacks regen ticks. A player with 1 Legendary + 1 Rare Nest gets 2 parallel ticks — potentially doubling refill speed vs a single nest.

Weekly treasury burn

Every week, the SOL Clash treasury wallet burns 30% of its $GOLD balance. Burned tokens are sent to a verifiable dead address on Solana, permanently removing them from the circulating supply. This mechanism creates consistent deflationary pressure over time, rewarding long-term holders and players who accumulate $GOLD through gameplay.

All burn transactions are on-chain and publicly verifiable. Burn events will be announced on official social channels with the transaction signature for transparency.

Earning $GOLD — how it works

During every Competitive run, $GOLD coins appear inside pipe gaps. Each pipe that has a $GOLD drop awards between 500 and 1,000 $GOLD when your bird passes through it. The spawn rate of gold coins per pipe depends on the Nests you own — better Nests produce gold coins more frequently.

Scheduled Gold Payout

$GOLD does not credit instantly to your wallet mid-game. Instead, a Scheduled Gold Payout system runs at a fixed time every day — 12:00 PM GMT+8. All $GOLD collected across your competitive runs during the day is tallied and automatically transferred to your generated Solana wallet at payout time. No manual claiming required.

🕛 Payout time: 12:00 PM GMT+8 daily. Any $GOLD you collect before that time is transferred automatically to your in-game wallet. You do not need to do anything — just play and wait for the scheduled transfer.

Multiple nests

You can own multiple Nests. Each one runs its own independent energy regen timer in parallel. Owning 3 Rare Nests, for example, generates 3 ticks per hour — tripling refill speed compared to a single Rare Nest. There is no cap on how many Nests you can own.

BP is determined by the rarest nest you own, not the total count. So owning 10 Common Nests still awards 21–40 BP per game; you need at least one Rare or Legendary to unlock higher BP ranges.

The Hatchery is a passive income layer. Place your upgraded Nests (Lvl 3+) into the Hatchery and they generate Eggs over time — no active gameplay required. Collect your Eggs when they're ready.

Access requirements

  • Nests can be placed and removed at any time from the Farm page.

Fair reward enforcement

All rewards are validated before being credited. A minimum cooldown is enforced between score submissions to prevent rapid-fire farming. Seeds collected per run are capped at 50 maximum regardless of in-game events. This ensures the reward economy stays balanced and fair for all players.
